About Us

Synchem is ethical formulation marketer committed to fighting Sickness and disease Through targeted and better medicines and information services to health-care providers, chiefly physicians.The company’s vision is that no patient shall be denied access to high quality & affordable medicine and support.The company's product line include more then 30 speciality formulation. Synchem was formed in 1984 as a result of untiring efforts of distinguished professionals having a two decades long exposure and experience in pharmacology, pharmaceutical production and distribution. The company fuctions from its corporate office in indore city and operates ultramodern manufacturing facilities in Baddi & Vadodara. Over the past one-and-a-half decades, Synchem has build up a strong presence in the Indian market.
